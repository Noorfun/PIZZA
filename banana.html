Banana Pizza Recipe

Welcome to the delicious world of Banana Pizza! This unique recipe combines the sweetness of bananas with the savory goodness of pizza. Perfect for adventurous eaters!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Brush the dough with olive oil to prevent it from getting soggy.
  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  7. Arrange the banana slices evenly on top of the cheese.
  8. If desired, sprinkle cinnamon over the bananas for added flavor.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  12. Slice and serve your delicious banana pizza warm!
