Cheese Bread

Start with a cheesy bread base, perfect for any pizza lover!

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 450Â°F (230Â°C).
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Sprinkle garlic powder, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Slice and enjoy your cheesy bread!
